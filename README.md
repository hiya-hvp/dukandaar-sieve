# 🛡️ दुकानdaar – The Autonomous Financial Guardian
**Redefining the Paytm ecosystem from a payment tool into a proactive Agentic AI co-pilot for India’s 42 million merchants.**

> *India's first Autonomous Financial Guardian for Small and Medium Businesses (SMBs). Moves beyond a standalone app or chatbot to act as a silent back-office employee that guards the phone, cleans the bills, and fuels the shop.*

---

## 📌 Overview
- **Target Users:** SMB merchants in India  
- **Goal:** Transform a transactional app into a **proactive intelligent co-pilot**  
- **Core Value:** Security, compliance, and seamless financial empowerment  
- **Integration:** Works directly with **Paytm for Business** and **Soundbox 4.0**

---

## ⚙️ Shield-Sieve-Sail Architecture
The system functions through a continuous autonomous loop using **three specialized AI engines**:

### 1️⃣ SHIELD: Emotion-Aware Security
- **Problem:** 1 in 3 UPI fraud victims are small merchants targeted while stressed or distracted on calls.  
- **Innovation:** Uses smartphone **accelerometer and gyroscope sensors** to detect **behavioral biometrics** (micro-tremors, touch pressure).  
- **Action:** On detecting stress during calls (*vishing signals*), automatically triggers a **120-second "Cooling-Off Pause"** with an audible alert:  
> "Ramesh-ji, you seem stressed. I have paused this for your safety."

---

### 2️⃣ SIEVE: Subscription Sentinel
- **Problem:** 67% of users face "subscription traps," and 52% encounter hidden fees that reduce profits.  
- **Innovation:** Autonomous **Ombudsman powered by GPT-4o** scans recurring payment terms in real-time.  
- **Action:** Detects "basket sneaking" or "drip pricing" and **blocks dishonest charges** before funds leave the account.  

---

### 3️⃣ SAIL: Contextual Credit Engine
- **Problem:** A **$280B credit gap** exists; 49M "thin-file" merchants lack formal tax history.  
- **Innovation:** Creates a **"Life Rhythm Score"** from transaction velocity and local festive/harvest cycles.  
- **Action:** Offers **"Invisible Credit"** (Merchant Cash Advances) proactively during festive rushes, with **one-tap disbursal** and repayment as a % of daily sales.

## 💻 Technical Architecture
Built for scale and resilience using **Microsoft-native stack**:

| Layer | Technology & Purpose |
|-------|--------------------|
| **Reasoning Engine** | Azure OpenAI (GPT-4o) – Legal payment term parsing & risk modeling |
| **Orchestration** | Microsoft AutoGen – Multi-agent workflow (Velocity → Risk → Credit Offer) |
| **Serverless Backend** | Azure Functions – Handle 1.3 crore Soundbox pings at peak load |
| **Stream Processing** | Apache Kafka – Millisecond-level transaction analysis |
| **Mobile Interface** | Flutter + Android Sensor Manager / iOS CoreMotion – Biometric sensing |

## 🔐 Privacy & Compliance

- **Risk Mitigation:** AI-driven scenario modeling prevents over-lending using local competition data.
